About

Welcome to my space on the web, where I’ll share insights from my software development journey, discuss projects, and provide links to the apps I’ve built.

I hold a computer science degree. I have been professionally developing software for over a decade. My focus is primarily on application and app development in C#. Prior to that, I spent another decade as a hobbyist creating games in C++—a formative experience that shaped my technical foundation and problem-solving approach.

This blog will serve as a platform for exploring a range of topics, including:

  • Codeless development: Investigating tools and workflows that streamline application creation without traditional coding.
  • AI integration and prompt engineering: Examining how to effectively incorporate artificial intelligence into software systems.
  • Game development: Revisiting my roots to build and analyze games from both a technical and creative perspective.

Whether you’re here for technical deep dives or to follow my ongoing projects, I hope you find something that resonates.
